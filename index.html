About me

From sketch to stitch, I design pieces that carry emotion. My work blends cultural storytelling, fine tailoring, and modern elegance.

Design philosophy

Every garment is a narrative: fabric, form, and detail working together. I prioritize fit, movement, and texture—so the wearer feels both calm and powerful.

Craft & process

Short, collaborative sprints with clients; thoughtful fittings; and ethical sourcing. Expect clarity, honest timelines, and pieces that last.

Signature

Hand embroidery, structured drapes, minimal palettes with amber and emerald accents.

Sizes

Custom couture and standard ready-to-wear across inclusive size ranges.
